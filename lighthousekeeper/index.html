Rules for The Lighthouse Keeper

Goal: Survive the storm by keeping the lighthouse's light lit until the entire storm deck is empty.

How to Play:

  1. Each round, a Storm Card is drawn. This is your challenge.
  2. The card has a Threat (what happens if you fail) and a Requirement (what action cards you must play to succeed).
  3. Choose cards from your hand that match the requirements and press "Play Selected Cards".
  4. If you cannot or will not meet the requirement, press "Endure the Threat" to accept the threat.
  5. Regardless of your choice, the storm card's Aftermath effect will occur.
  6. You lose if your Light Strength reaches 0. You win if you survive the last storm card.

Game Over

Victory or defeat message.
