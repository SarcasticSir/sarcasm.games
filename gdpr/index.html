Privacy at sarcasm.games

What we store

Registered users can submit a contact message through sarcasm.games. When they do, we store the name, reply email, and message content they submit.

Account and login data

We process account-related information needed for login and authentication so users can access their account and protected features.

How data is used

Data may be used to operate the service, keep the site secure, and respond to contact messages sent by users.

Privacy questions

If you have privacy questions, please use the contact page at sarcasm.games/contact after signing in.
